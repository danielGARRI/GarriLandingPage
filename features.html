How It Works

Garri makes freight logistics easier than ever before for Shippers, Transporters, and Drivers!

How Does Garri Work For Shippers?

Transport cargo anywhere in Ethiopia. We offer reliability, competitive pricing, and complete transparency. You can even track your cargo live from your mobile!!

  1. 1
    Get a quote

    Request a quote using our website, shipper app, or by calling us at (+251)-911-217-492

  2. 2
    Assign Transporter

    After agreeing on a price, Garri will match your shipment with the best transporter for the job.

  3. 3
    Move Cargo

    You will be notified when a driver is assigned to your load, and can track delivery progress in real time via the app or website.

  4. 4
    Complete Trip

    On delivery, you will receive trip documents and delivery confirmation.

How Does Garri Work For Transporters?

Get loads effortlessly. Get paid on time. Manage drivers and vehicles from your phone.

  1. 1
    Register

    Use our transporter app to register your company, trucks, and drivers. Complete inspection and insurance verification. Optionally install our GPS trackers on your trucks for monitoring.

  2. 2
    Find Load

    Use transporter app to find, review, bid on, and accept loads.

  3. 3
    Dispatch Driver, Monitor Progress

    Get paid a down payment immediately, and use the transporter app to assign a driver and monitor load status.

  4. 4
    Move Cargo

    Track your driver in real time as they deliver the load, while getting notified of any relevant trip updates.

How Does Garri Work For Drivers?

Get jobs quickly. Get paid instantly. Minimize inefficiency.

  1. 1
    Register

    Sign up with our driver app, submit license for verification. Your transporter will select you as a driver in their app.

  2. 2
    Find Job

    Work on jobs assigned by transporters, or find our own.

  3. 3
    Pick Up Load

    Driver to pickup location at scheduled time, and pick up load.

  4. 4
    Complete Job

    Drive to dropoff location, deliver load, and upload accompanying documents. Get paid immediately on completion.
